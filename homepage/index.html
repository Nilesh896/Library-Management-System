About Us

We are a team of four second-year engineering students: Nilesh Yadav, Akshay Yadav, Sachin Yadav and Mihir Singh. Our project, the Library Management System (LMS), is designed to modernize the way libraries function by integrating digital resources and streamlining operations. Key features include the addition of e-books, audiobooks, and a dedicated section for Previous Year Questions (PYQs), making it easier for students to access a wide range of learning materials. The LMS also manages physical books, providing real-time availability and automated notifications for due dates or overdue items. Our system enhances the user experience by incorporating advanced search capabilities and personalized user accounts, which allow users to track borrowed items and access digital resources with ease. By blending traditional library functions with digital innovations, we aim to create a comprehensive platform that caters to the diverse learning needs of students and faculty alike. This project not only helps in better resource management but also contributes to improving the efficiency of academic preparation.
